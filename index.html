Company Profile

Vavoom Trading Limited is a dynamic company incorporated as a private limited company by shares on 2nd October, 2024 (Certificate of Incorporation number 120241013518). Based in Lusaka, we are registered with all relevant regulatory bodies including the Patents and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA) and Zambia Public Procurement Agency (ZPPA).

Founded by Paul Tembo and Bridget Ng'andwe Kambobe, Vavoom Trading is rapidly establishing itself as a leading supplier in the manufacturing sector, specializing in the transportation and storage of raw materials.

Our comprehensive service portfolio extends beyond logistics to include construction, mining and quarrying, wholesale and retail trade, and motor vehicle repairs - making us a versatile partner for diverse business needs.

At Vavoom, we combine industry expertise with innovative solutions to deliver exceptional value to our clients while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.
